Roommate Story

This is my roommate since the first day of my diploma. She has shared a room with me from Semester 1 until Semester 5. Even though we faced a bit of a setback in Semester 4, when she got a room on a lower floor as an MPP, we still managed to stay in the same block. During that time, I still made it a point to meet and catch up with her, and we continue to do so until now.

From the beginning until now, she has been the only person who truly knows the changes I have gone through over the semesters. Whenever I face a problem, she is always the first person willing to listen and support me. Even though we are in the same course but different classes, our bond has stayed very strong.

She is also a great cook and often reminds me to eat. Sometimes we cook and share meals together, which strengthens our friendship. I feel a little sad knowing that this is our last semester together, and I wonder if we will have the chance to live like this again during our degree. Her presence has taught me independence, patience, and teamwork, and I am truly grateful to have her in my life.
