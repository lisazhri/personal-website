People I Love

Family | Sister | High School Friends | Roommate

Family

I come from a family of six siblings, and I am the second child. My father works full-time, while my mother is a full-time housewife. They have always supported me in my interests. My fifth and sixth siblings are non-identical twins, making our family even more special. Before entering university, my mother encouraged me to work part-time, helping me face my fear of communicating with others.

Sister

My sister is two years older than me. We used to argue when younger due to different personalities, but now we catch up and share stories whenever she returns home. She has encouraged me to grow as a person, offering advice and support. We often stay up late talking, laughing, and sometimes crying together, and I truly value her presence in my life.

My Lovely Mokara

These friends have been with me since Form 1. Mokara has been the name of our class since day one. Even though some of us were separated in Form 3, our friendship has stayed strong. I really appreciate their presence and love catching up, sharing life updates, and reminiscing about fun moments. They will always have a special place in my heart… can’t wait to see who will get married first

Roommate

She has been my roommate from Semester 1 to Semester 5. Living together taught me independence and patience, and we were actually in the same major, just in different classes. Even though we had to move to different floors in Semester 4 because she was an MPP at that time, we still made it a point to catch up at least once a week to keep each other updated on life. She’s always been there for me, and I feel bit sad since we were almost always together during our diploma journey. Thank you for being such a great friend, my beloved Alia Zulaikha.
